# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone**: Enthusiastic & Dramatic. Documentation and UI labels should evoke the energy of a superhero comic. Use bold verbs and expressive language where appropriate (e.g., "ASSEMBLE!" instead of "Submit").
- **Language**: Use Marvel-specific terminology (e.g., "Datafile", "Watcher", "PP") consistently.

## Branding & Visual Direction
- **Theme**: Silver Age Retro.
- **Design Tokens**:
    - **Palette**: Use primary comic colors (Red, Blue, Yellow) as base accents. 
    - **Typography**: `Bangers` for headers and dramatic labels; `Oswald` (italicized) for labels and data values to mimic classic comic fonts.
- **UI Elements**: High-contrast black borders (4px), solid retro drop shadows (`shadow-comic`), and vibrant panel backgrounds.

## UX Principles
- **Tactile Interaction**: Clicking dice should feel responsive. Ensure smooth cycling of die types (d4-d12).
- **Speed of Play**: Minimize clicks. Navigation between the Datafile and the Assembler should be instantaneous.
- **Mobile-First Optimization**: Maintain a single-column layout on small screens. Ensure all touch targets (dice icons, buttons) are large and easily tappable.
- **Feedback**: Provide immediate visual confirmation for actions, such as "pop" animations for new dice and color changes for active states.
